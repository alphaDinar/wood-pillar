Welcome

Incorporated in the year 1967, Wood Pillar LTD, a Controlled Wood(CW) & Chain of Custody(COC) Certified company in the timber processing industry, provides a wide range of high quality finished and semi-finished, wood products in several of the finest species. Wood Pillar LTD is the leader in the timber processing industry in Ghana. Our Slicer, Sawmill and Moulding departments provide a wide range of high quality products from numerous species with the ability to meet the most challenging specifications.

In order to resuscitate some of the already degraded forests and for the company to be self-reliant and consistent in the timber business, vigorous reforestation projects are being embarked upon. It is the view of the company that planted forests can equally provide an array of social, economic and environmental benefits, and can contribute to satisfying the world’s needs for forest products
In order to demonstrate the company’s commitment to protecting conservation values in its forests, the company has trained its stock survey team on how to identify HCVs. This led to the identification of the bareheaded rock fowl in the Nkrabea Forest Reserve.
Wood Pillar LTD considers re-afforestation a key aspect of its operations. A total area of over 1200ha is being transformed into a forest;
