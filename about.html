About Us

Wood Pillar LTD is among the largest timbers and sawmills companies in Ghana with five main operating divisions: tree nurseries, forestry, sawmilling, timber manufacturing and energy. Wood Pillar LTD manufactures and supplies a wide range of quality sawn products and moulding products for both exports and for local consumption. Wood Pillar LTD employs some of the very latest equipment in its daily operations. Wood Pillar LTD Supplies Controlled Wood.
